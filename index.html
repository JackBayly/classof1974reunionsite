DHS ALUMNI PLAZA

Let’s create history together!

The DHS Class of 1974 is crafting a lasting tribute—an Alumni Marble Bench, which will become a part of the Walk of Benches in the DHS Alumni Plaza.. This bench represents our shared experiences and bonds from our time at Decatur High. We invite your generosity to bring this vision to life.

Your support will etch our legacy in stone and create a spot for future generations of Decatur High students to gather, share stories, and forge friendships just like ours.

Building and designing the bench will take approximately 4 months. Once installed, a dedication service will be held and classmates will be notified when the date has been set.

With a cost of $2,154.60, your donation, big or small, is invaluable. To contribute, mail a Check or Money Order to DHS Class of 1974 Reunion, P. O. Box 362103, Decatur, GA 30036, or Zelle your donation to 1974dhs50@gmail.com, specifying it's for the Marble Bench.

Every contribution counts—let's make this dream a reality together!
